Home Assistant Integration

  1. Open HACS (Home Assistant Community Store) in your Home Assistant instance.
  2. Click the three dots (top right), select Custom repositories.
  3. Add https://github.com/pkreissel/temperature-crowd-hacs with the category Integration.
  4. Search for TemperaturCrowd in HACS and click Download.
  5. Restart Home Assistant.
  6. Navigate to Settings > Devices & Services > Add Integration, search for TemperaturCrowd, and follow the setup.
